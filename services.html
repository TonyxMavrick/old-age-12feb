Our Care Services

Person-centered care tailored to each resident's unique needs, delivered with compassion, dignity, and professional excellence.

Assisted Living

Support with daily tasks in a private, home-like setting

  • Personal care assistance with bathing, dressing, and grooming
  • Medication management by licensed nurses
  • Housekeeping and laundry services
  • Private or semi-private suites with modern amenities
  • 24/7 on-call support

Memory Care

Specialized, secure environment for those with Alzheimer's or Dementia

  • Secure, calming spaces designed to reduce confusion
  • Staff trained in dementia care best practices
  • Personalized memory engagement activities
  • Nutritional support for cognitive health
  • Family education and support groups

Respite Care

Short-term stays for families needing temporary support

  • Flexible stays from a few days to several weeks
  • Full access to all community amenities
  • Professional care to give family caregivers a break
  • Trial period option for those considering long-term care
  • Seamless transition support

Holistic Wellness

Comprehensive programs nurturing body, mind, and spirit

  • Physiotherapy and occupational therapy
  • Gentle yoga and tai chi classes
  • Art therapy and creative expression
  • Pet therapy with certified therapy animals
  • Meditation and mindfulness sessions
